CheerpJ + FreeJ2ME

Trinh gia lap J2ME ngay trong trinh duyet

Upload file .jar/.jad, tinh chinh kich thuoc man hinh, luu du lieu cuc bo va chia se nhanh game Java Mobile.

Ho tro desktop & mobile

Launcher nam trong khung tren. Neu muon toan man hinh, bam nut fullscreen cua trinh duyet hoac mo run.html o tab moi.

Huong dan nhanh

  1. Upload .jar: Bam "Select JAR file" va (neu can) them file JAD.
  2. Tinh chinh: Chon do phan giai, font, phone type, bat/tat am thanh hoac 3D.
  3. Luu & chia se: "Export Data" tao goi zip; dat file app_id.zip vao thu muc freej2me-web/web/apps de nap nhanh.
  4. Chay game: Bam ten game trong danh sach, hoac truy cap freej2me-web/web/run.html?app=ID.

Phim tat

Esc mo menu tuy chinh; F1/F2 la phim mem trai/phai; OK la Enter; phim so map nhu dien thoai; pad ao tu bat tren mobile.

Dung nut Maximize trong khung choi de doi scale, hoac nut fullscreen cua trinh duyet de choi thoai mai hon.

Meo embed

Sau khi export, tao appid.zip va dat trong thu muc apps. URL mau: freej2me-web/web/run.html?app=appid&fractionScale=1.

Neu mo tren di dong, them mobile=1 de bat ban phim ao toi uu cam ung.
